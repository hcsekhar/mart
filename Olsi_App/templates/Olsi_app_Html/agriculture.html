Agriculture and DRY NUTS Prospects


Agricultural Landscape in Numbers

India's agriculture employs around 50% of its workforce. It contributes about 15% to the country's GDP. The sector faces challenges like soil degradation, water scarcity, and climate variability.

Fertilizer Woes

Over 60% of Indian soils suffer from nutrient deficiency. Traditional fertilizer practices often lead to imbalanced soil health. Excessive use of chemical fertilizers impacts environment and crop quality.

Pain Points Addressed by DRY NUTS's Agriproducts

DRY NUTS's agriproducts offer sustainable alternatives to traditional fertilizers. They focus on enhancing soil health and nutrient content. Precision application minimizes excess fertilizer usage, reducing environmental impact

The DRY NUTS Approach

DRY NUTS's agriproducts are research-backed, tailored to Indian soil conditions. They are designed to improve crop yield, quality, and overall sustainability. By promoting balanced soil health, DRY NUTS aids in mitigating fertilizer-related issues.

Impact and Future

By addressing fertilizer challenges, they align with the vision of a sustainable agricultural future. As Indian agriculture navigates complexities, DRY NUTS's commitment to innovation and sustainable practices through its agriproducts paves the way for a more resilient and prosperous farming sector.
